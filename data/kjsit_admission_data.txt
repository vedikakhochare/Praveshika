2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR FIRST YEAR ADMISSION

Maharashtra Candidates:
• Indian national
• Passed HSC with Physics & Maths (+ one approved subject) with 45% marks (40% for reserved/PwD/EWS candidates)
• Non-zero CET score required
• OR Diploma in Engineering with 45% marks (40% for reserved candidates) / 50% marks (45% for reserved candidates)

All India Candidates:
• Indian national
• Passed HSC with Physics & Maths (+ one approved subject) with 45% marks (40% for reserved candidates)
• Non-zero JEE Main score (preferred) or non-zero CET score required


3. ADMISSION PROCESS FOR FIRST YEAR

Admissions are strictly merit-based as per rules of DTE & Admission Regulating Authority, Govt. of Maharashtra.

All seats are filled through CAP (Centralized Admission Process) conducted by State CET Cell as per schedule.

If seats remain vacant after CAP:
1. Apply online via the college website: https://kjsit.somaiya.edu.in/en
2. Submit the form online with required documents and pay ₹1000 processing fee
3. Vacant seat notifications are published in newspapers and on the college website & notice board
4. Candidates must have applied earlier to the Competent Authority and completed document verification at an E-Scrutiny Center

Refer to the college website for application forms, prospectus, deadlines, merit lists, and counselling schedule.

6. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR FIRST YEAR ADMISSION

Documents Checklist for the First Year Engineering:
• One true copy of required documents must be submitted at the time of submitting application form printed from our website
• Candidate must bring original for verification at the time of submission of printed application form
• Three sets of true copies along with originals of required documents must be submitted at the time of confirming admission

For all candidates:
• SSC (Std X) Mark sheet
• HSC (Std XII) Mark sheet
• MHT-CET Score Card 2024
• School Leaving Certificate after passing HSC (Std XII)
• Migration Certificate (For candidates other than Maharashtra State Board)
• Certificate of Indian Nationality
• True copy of Aadhar card (at the time of admission)
• Gap affidavit (If Year of passing HSC before 2022)

For candidates admitted through CAP:
• CAP Allotment letter (at the time of admission)
• Receipt-cum Acknowledgement from FC / ARC (at the time of admission)
• In addition to the above mentioned documents, the candidates admitted under different categories in CAP rounds are required to submit documents as mentioned in information brochure issued by competent authority

For All India (JEE Main 2024 Paper-I) Candidates:
• Scorecard of JEE (Main) 2024 Paper-I

For SC/ST Candidates:
• Caste Certificate from competent Authority
• Caste Validity Certificate (Compulsory as per notification received from Govt. of Maharashtra)
• Income Certificate issued by competent authority
• Ration Card
• Bank Account details

For NT1, NT2, NT3, OBC & SBC Candidates:
• Caste Certificate from competent Authority
• Non-creamy layer certificate valid up to 31st March, 2024
• Caste Validity Certificate (Compulsory as per notification received from Govt. of Maharashtra)
• Income Certificate issued by competent authority
• Ration Card
• Bank Account details

For Gujarati Linguistic Minority Candidates:
• School Leaving Certificate mentioning Gujarati Language as mother tongue
• Certificate issued by registered community regarding mother tongue of the candidate
• Affidavit on stamp paper of Rs. 100/- in prescribed format (Available on college website)
• Domicile Certificate of Maharashtra State (or documents specified for Maharashtra State Type A or Type B candidate)
