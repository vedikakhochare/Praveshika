ENGINEERING COLLEGE ADMISSION INFORMATION

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 12th standard (or equivalent)
- Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
- Valid entrance exam score (JEE/CET/MHT-CET) required
- Age limit: Maximum 25 years as on admission date

ADMISSION PROCESS:
1. Centralized Admission Process (CAP): Apply through official CAP portal
2. Institute Level Admission: Direct admission based on merit and availability
3. Documents verification at college campus
4. Fee payment and seat confirmation

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- 12th standard marksheet (original + 2 copies)
- 10th standard marksheet (original + 2 copies)
- Entrance exam scorecard
- Transfer certificate from previous institution
- Migration certificate (if applicable)
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Income certificate (for fee concession)
- Passport size photographs (4 copies)
- Aadhar card copy
- Medical fitness certificate

FEE STRUCTURE:
- Tuition Fee: Rs. 50,000 per year
- Development Fee: Rs. 10,000 per year
- Laboratory Fee: Rs. 5,000 per year
- Library Fee: Rs. 2,000 per year
- Total: Rs. 67,000 per year

AVAILABLE BRANCHES:
- Computer Engineering (120 seats)
- Electronics Engineering (60 seats)
- Mechanical Engineering (60 seats)
- Civil Engineering (60 seats)

CUTOFF MARKS (Previous Year):
- Computer Engineering: 85% (CAP), 80% (Institute Level)
- Electronics Engineering: 75% (CAP), 70% (Institute Level)
- Mechanical Engineering: 70% (CAP), 65% (Institute Level)
- Civil Engineering: 65% (CAP), 60% (Institute Level)

ADMISSION DATES:
- CAP Application: June 1 - June 30
- CAP Merit List: July 15
- Institute Level Application: July 20 - August 10
- Classes Begin: August 15
